Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

Kitties4Lyfe ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application and services ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize Kitties4Lyfe via TikTok, we collect:

  • TikTok Open ID — A unique identifier provided by TikTok to identify your account.
  • OAuth Access Tokens — Used to publish content to your TikTok account on your behalf.
  • Refresh Tokens — Used to maintain your authorization without requiring repeated logins.

We do not collect your TikTok password, personal messages, follower lists, or any content from your TikTok account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Publishing Content — We use your access token solely to publish AI-generated cat videos to your TikTok account.
  • Maintaining Authorization — We use refresh tokens to keep your session active.
  • We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or analytics beyond basic service operation.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • Your data is only transmitted to TikTok's API as required to provide the Service.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth tokens are stored securely on our servers and are encrypted at rest.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
  • Access to stored data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Data Retention

  • We retain your OAuth tokens only as long as your TikTok authorization is active.
  • When you revoke access or disconnect the app, we delete your tokens within 30 days.
  • You may request immediate deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • Revoke Access — You can disconnect Kitties4Lyfe from your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's settings.
  • Data Deletion — You may request deletion of all data we hold about you.
  • Data Access — You may request a copy of the data we store about you.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at kitties4lyfe@proton.me.
